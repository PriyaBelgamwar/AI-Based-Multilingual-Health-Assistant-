About Our Project

The AI Symptom Checker is a simple yet powerful virtual health assistant designed to help users—especially rural and remote communities—understand their symptoms and get quick guidance.

Many people struggle to access reliable healthcare information due to long distances, lack of hospitals, or language barriers. Our platform provides an easy-to-use interface where users can describe their symptoms in English, Hindi, or Marathi, and receive instant AI-based suggestions.

The system also helps users find nearby doctors, clinics, and hospitals based on live location, making it extremely helpful for emergency and rural healthcare needs.

This prototype is designed for educational and research purposes as part of a final-year engineering project, demonstrating how Artificial Intelligence and geolocation technology can make healthcare more accessible.
